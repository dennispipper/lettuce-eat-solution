Lettuce Eat is a local company started by a local vision - in a perfect world, the ideal food is to be nutritious & delicious. What started out as a personal desire for quality vegetable & meat options, has turned into thriving "Fast Casual" dining. We take great pride in offering only the freshest foods and maintaining five-start quality ratings. .

Lettuce Eat is open for lunch and dinner, to dine in or carry out.
